    AI analysis and follow-up chat
    The RAG Abstraction: Big AI systems (like ChatGPT or Copilot) don't memorize everything. Instead, they use a two-step framework called Retrieval-Augmented Generation to answer questions accurately and avoid hallucinating facts. Here is how we built it locally.
    1. Embed & Retrieve
    We turn text into numbers. The E5 model converts your query and PDFs into 768-dimensional vectors (embeddings). We then calculate the Cosine Similarity—the angle between these vectors—to pull the absolute top 5 contextual matches.
    2. Augment & Generate
    The retrieved text isn't just shown to you; it's fed to Gemini as direct evidence. By grounding the LLM with this specific context, it generates highly accurate answers based only on your documents, identical to how enterprise AI operates.
    3. The Tech Stack
    Built with a Flask API serving a pure vanilla JavaScript/CSS frontend, avoiding heavy frameworks. It includes a robust backend mechanism that automatically fails-over across 4 different Gemini API keys and models to guarantee uptime.
